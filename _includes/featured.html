Knowingly and unknowingly we are always trying to bring order to our lives by making "Lists", but we forget that "Plot Twists" are the rule of nature!!

Weaving these twists into fabric of life while managing the ever growing lists is the narrative that forms central identity of women in this “Blog and Podcast series”

TThis is our attempt to offer more than just a glimpse into lives of these ordinary yet extraordinary women, who manage the orderly and disorderly stories on both personal and professional fronts.
